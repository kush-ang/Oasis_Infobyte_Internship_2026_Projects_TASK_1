Task 1: Retail Sales Analysis In this project, I analyzed a dataset of 1,000 retail transactions to identify shopping trends, seasonal performance, and gender-based spending patterns using Power BI.

Key Insights Found: Total Revenue: $456K with an average transaction value of $456.

Best Month: May saw the highest revenue ($58,590).

Weak Months: December, September, and March had the lowest sales performance.

Gender Analysis: Females contribute 51.06% ($233K) of total revenue.

Key Finding: Female spending drops significantly in February and November, representing a growth opportunity for targeted marketing.

Top Category: Electronics is the most demanded product category.
